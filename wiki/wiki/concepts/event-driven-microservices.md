# Event-Driven Microservices

## Definition

An integration style in which loosely coupled services communicate by publishing and subscribing to asynchronous messages (e.g. via Redis pub/sub or a broker), temporally decoupling producers and consumers to avoid a 'distributed ball of mud'.
