# Generative Computing

## Definition

A proposed compute paradigm in which neuron-based LLMs join classical (bits) and quantum (qubits) computing as a building block, with structured prompts, runtimes, memory slots, and KV-cache management replacing today's ad-hoc 'mega-prompt' style of prompt engineering.
