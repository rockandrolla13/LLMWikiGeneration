# Foundation Models

## Definition

Large, pre-trained models (including LLMs and large multimodal models) that serve as a general-purpose base for many downstream applications via adaptation rather than training from scratch. Huyen frames them as the substrate that turned ML from a specialised discipline into a broadly accessible development tool.
