# Sampling Strategies

## Definition

Decoding-time mechanisms — greedy, temperature, top-k, top-p (nucleus), min-p, beam search, and best-of-N — for selecting tokens from a language model's probability distribution. Huyen connects them to the probabilistic nature of foundation models and to test-time compute as a lever for response quality.
