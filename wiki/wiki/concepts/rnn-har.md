# RNN-HAR

**RNN-HAR** is the hybrid Peiris-Tran-Wang-Gerlach (2025) model embedding daily/weekly/monthly RNNs inside the HAR cascade and estimating directly the conditional quantile of returns via asymmetric-Laplace quantile loss.
