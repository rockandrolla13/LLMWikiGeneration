# LLM-as-a-Judge

## Definition

Evaluation pattern where a separate LLM call scores the output of an application against a reference or criterion, used for offline and online evaluation of LLM apps. The book covers calibrating LLM-as-a-judge with few-shot human corrections to align it with human preferences.
