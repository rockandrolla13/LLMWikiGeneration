# AI Agents

## Definition

Systems in which a foundation model plans and executes multi-step tasks by invoking tools, observing outputs, reflecting, and revising its plan. Huyen covers tool design, planning failure modes (invalid tool, invalid parameters, goal failure), agent memory, and agent-specific evaluation.
