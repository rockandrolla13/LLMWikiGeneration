# Shapley Values

**Shapley Values** are a game-theoretic attribution of a model's prediction across features by averaging marginal contributions over all feature subsets; standard tool for ML interpretability in finance.

- [[sources/cotturo-2026-multifactor-timing-deep-learning|Multifactor Timing with Deep Learning (2026)]] — supporting; identifies unemployment, leverage, profitability, and money as the most influential DMT predictors
