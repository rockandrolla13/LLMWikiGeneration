# Jailbreaking

## Definition

The practice of crafting adversarial prompts that bypass an LLM's alignment, content filters, or safety guardrails to coerce it into producing disallowed content or executing forbidden actions. Closely related to prompt injection but typically targets the model's policy layer rather than downstream application logic.
