# Stochastic Conditional Intensity

**Stochastic Conditional Intensity (SCI)** is the Bauwens-Hautsch class of point-process models where the intensity is modulated by a latent stochastic component; here extended to panels of investors and assets.

## Sources

- [[sources/nolte-2011-fx-latent-factor-panel-intensity|Trading Dynamics in the FX Market: A Latent Factor Panel Intensity Approach (2011)]] — central; the latent stochastic factor drives time-variation in trader-action intensities
