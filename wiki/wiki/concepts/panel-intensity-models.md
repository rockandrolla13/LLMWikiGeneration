# Panel Intensity Models

**Panel Intensity Models** are continuous-time hazard models for multi-investor, multi-asset event sequences combining individual fixed effects, time-varying covariates, seasonality, and latent factors.

## Sources

- [[sources/nolte-2011-fx-latent-factor-panel-intensity|Trading Dynamics in the FX Market: A Latent Factor Panel Intensity Approach (2011)]] — central; the modeling framework for joint open/close decisions across FX investors
