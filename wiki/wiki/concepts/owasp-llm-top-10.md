# OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications

## Definition

A community-driven list, launched in 2023 and led by the author, enumerating the most critical security risks and vulnerabilities specific to applications that incorporate large language models. It adapts the OWASP Top 10 methodology to LLM-specific threats such as prompt injection, training data poisoning, insecure output handling, and supply chain attacks.
