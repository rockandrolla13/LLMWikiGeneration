# Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning

## Definition

A family of techniques (LoRA, adapters, prompt-tuning, InstructLab-style alignment) that update only a small subset of a model's parameters or auxiliary modules, allowing enterprises to specialise large pretrained models on proprietary data without full retraining.
