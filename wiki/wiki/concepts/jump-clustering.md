# Jump Clustering

**Jump Clustering** is the empirical tendency for asset-return jumps to arrive in clustered sequences rather than independently, often following news; quantified via self-excitation parameters in Hawkes-driven jump models.

- [[sources/chen-2024-jump-clustering-information-flows|Jump Clustering, Information Flows, and Stock Price Efficiency (2024)]] — central; proposed as a new measure of stock price inefficiency
