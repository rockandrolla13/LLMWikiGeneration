# Parameter-Efficient Finetuning

## Definition

Finetuning strategies that update only a small fraction of a model's parameters — including LoRA-style low-rank adapters and prompt/prefix tuning — to adapt foundation models with bounded memory and storage cost. Discussed alongside model merging and multi-task finetuning as the practical alternative to full finetuning.
