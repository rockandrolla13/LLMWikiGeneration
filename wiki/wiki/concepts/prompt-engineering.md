# Prompt Engineering

## Definition

The practice of designing, iterating on, and defending inputs to foundation models — including system/user prompts, in-context examples, instructions, and structural cues — to elicit desired behaviour without modifying model weights. Covers best practices, versioning, and defensive techniques against prompt injection and jailbreaking.
