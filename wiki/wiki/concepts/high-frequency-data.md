# High-Frequency Data

**High-Frequency Data** are intraday price observations sampled at minute or sub-minute frequency; basis for realized measures of variance, covariance, and probability indices.

- [[sources/xie-2026-realized-probability-market-timing|Realized Probability Index is a Better Market Timing Indicator (2026)]] — supporting; the realized probability index is constructed directly from high-frequency intraday returns
