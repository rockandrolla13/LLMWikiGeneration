# Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## Definition

A prompting technique introduced by Google researchers (Wei et al., 2022) that improves LLM reasoning by decomposing a problem into intermediate steps, used in coding to walk a model through requirement, design, and implementation actions sequentially.
