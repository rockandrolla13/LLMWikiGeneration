# AI Value Creator

## Definition

An enterprise consumption pattern in which a business uses an AI platform to combine its proprietary data, governance, and large language models to build differentiated, owned AI solutions, in contrast with the 'AI User' who merely consumes someone else's embedded or hosted AI.
