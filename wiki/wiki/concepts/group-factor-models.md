# Group Factor Models

**Group Factor Models** are factor models for two or more panels with both common factors spanning all groups and group-specific (idiosyncratic-to-group) factors orthogonal to the common ones.

## Sources

- [[sources/andreou-2020-mixed-frequency-macro-finance|Mixed-Frequency Macro-Finance Factor Models (2020)]] — central; provides asymptotic theory for testing common and group-specific factors across macroeconomic and financial panels
