# LangGraph

## Definition

Graph-based orchestration library from the LangChain team for building stateful, cyclical LLM applications. Applications are modelled as a StateGraph of nodes and edges, with shared state, checkpointing, and built-in support for memory, multi-agent systems, subgraphs, and human-in-the-loop control.
