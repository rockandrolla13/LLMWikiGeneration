# Nonparametric Conditional Moment Test

## Definition

A kernel-based specification test (in the tradition of Fan-Li 1996 and Li 1999) that checks whether a conditional moment restriction holds, used here to detect significance of auxiliary variables for the conditional mean of policy choice.
