# Efficient Importance Sampling

**Efficient Importance Sampling (EIS)** is the Richard-Zhang adaptive Monte Carlo method for evaluating high-dimensional integrals arising in simulated maximum likelihood estimation of latent-factor models.

- [[sources/nolte-2011-fx-latent-factor-panel-intensity|Trading Dynamics in the FX Market: A Latent Factor Panel Intensity Approach (2011)]] — central; enables tractable estimation of the latent-factor panel intensity model
