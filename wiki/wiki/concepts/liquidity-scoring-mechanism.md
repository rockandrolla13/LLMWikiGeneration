# Liquidity Scoring Mechanism

## Definition

A proprietary ranking methodology scoring tradable instruments on cost and time to exit using observable inputs - dealer-quote frequency, participating dealers, weekly notional and trade count, bid/offer spread, dispersion, and volatility.
