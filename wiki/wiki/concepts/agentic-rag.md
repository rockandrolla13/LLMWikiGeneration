# Agentic RAG

## Definition

An evolution of RAG in which autonomous LLM agents orchestrate multi-step or iterative retrieval, invoke external tools (web search, APIs), decompose complex queries, and coordinate sub-agents. Adds reasoning and adaptability beyond a single retrieve-then-generate pass.
