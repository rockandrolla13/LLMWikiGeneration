# Bayesian Additive Regression Trees (BART)

## Definition

A Bayesian nonparametric sum-of-trees model in which many weak-learner regression trees are combined and regularized by a prior, with the posterior fit via MCMC, used here to flexibly model a response surface for causal effect estimation.
