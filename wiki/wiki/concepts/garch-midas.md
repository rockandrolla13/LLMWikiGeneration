# GARCH-MIDAS

**GARCH-MIDAS** is the Engle-Ghysels-Sohn model splitting variance into a short-run GARCH component and a long-run MIDAS component driven by macro variables sampled at low frequency.

- [[sources/boffelli-2017-euro-bond-spread-correlations|High- and Low-Frequency Correlations in European Government Bond Spreads (2017)]] — central; links bond-spread volatility to deteriorating macro fundamentals
