# Multivariate Realized Kernel

**Multivariate Realized Kernel** is a kernel-based estimator (Barndorff-Nielsen, Hansen, Lunde, Shephard 2011) of integrated covariance robust to microstructure noise and asynchronous trading; guaranteed positive semidefinite under Parzen kernels.
