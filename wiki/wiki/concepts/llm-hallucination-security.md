# Hallucination as a Security Risk

## Definition

The treatment of LLM hallucinations not merely as a quality issue but as a security and liability hazard, since fabricated citations, code, or facts can be inserted into legal filings, business applications, or critical decisions. The book frames hallucination alongside prompt injection as a top-tier LLM risk.
