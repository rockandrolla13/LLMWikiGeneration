# ETF Creation/Redemption Arbitrage

## Definition

A statistical-arbitrage strategy exploiting mispricing between a corporate bond ETF and its underlying basket using the authorized-participant create/redeem mechanism; largely market-neutral and orthogonal to credit beta.
