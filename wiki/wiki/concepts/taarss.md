# Tactical Asset Allocation Relative Strength Signal (TAARSS)

## Definition

Deutsche Bank's proprietary signal scoring each asset class's ETF flow trend as the product of magnitude (regression-through-origin slope on flows-as-%-of-AUM) and path consistency (R-squared), z-scored cross-sectionally across 181 segments.
