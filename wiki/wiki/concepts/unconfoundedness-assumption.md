# Conditional Unconfoundedness / Conditional Independence Assumption

## Definition

The identifying assumption that, conditional on a set of predetermined controls, treatment (policy) assignment is independent of potential outcomes, so treatment is 'as good as randomly assigned'. Required for IPW identification of treatment effects.
