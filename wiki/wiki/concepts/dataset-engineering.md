# Dataset Engineering

## Definition

The systematic curation, acquisition, annotation, synthesis, and processing of data for adapting foundation models, covering quality, coverage, quantity, deduplication, filtering, AI-powered synthesis, and model distillation. Huyen elevates it to a first-class engineering discipline rather than a preparatory step.
