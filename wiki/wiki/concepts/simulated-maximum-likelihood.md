# Simulated Maximum Likelihood

**Simulated Maximum Likelihood (SML)** is likelihood estimation in latent-variable models via Monte Carlo or importance-sampling approximations to intractable integrals; foundational for SCI-type intensity models.

## Sources

- [[sources/nolte-2011-fx-latent-factor-panel-intensity|Trading Dynamics in the FX Market: A Latent Factor Panel Intensity Approach (2011)]] — supporting; combined with EIS for efficient estimation of the latent-factor panel model
