# Incomplete-Markets Heterogeneous-Agent (Bewley) Models

## Definition

Models restricting tradable assets, generating a precautionary savings motive and a nondegenerate wealth distribution; includes Aiyagari, Huggett, and Krusell-Smith extensions.
