# Private Credit / Direct Lending

## Definition

Non-bank lending to corporations via direct-lending funds and BDCs; the note forecasts direct-lending defaults rising to 8% and introduces 'Private Credit 2.0' expanding into infrastructure, real estate debt, and asset-based finance.
