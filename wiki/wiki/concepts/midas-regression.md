# MIDAS Regression

**MIDAS Regression** is a Mixed-Data Sampling regression of Ghysels et al. linking low-frequency dependent variables to high-frequency regressors via parsimonious lag-weighting polynomials (Beta, Almon).

## Sources

- [[sources/andreou-2020-mixed-frequency-macro-finance|Mixed-Frequency Macro-Finance Factor Models (2020)]] — supporting; used to aggregate high-frequency financial information for macro factor estimation
- [[sources/boffelli-2017-euro-bond-spread-correlations|High- and Low-Frequency Correlations in European Government Bond Spreads (2017)]] — central; basis of DCC-MIDAS and GARCH-MIDAS decompositions
