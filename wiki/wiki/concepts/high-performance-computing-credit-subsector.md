# High Performance Computing Credit Subsector

## Definition

A high-yield (and emerging leveraged-loan) credit subsector capturing AI/GPU compute and data-center issuers; grew from 1.07% to 2.68% of the JPMorgan HY Bond index in 2026 and led credit alpha.
