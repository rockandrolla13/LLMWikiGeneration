# Sensitive Data Disclosure in LLMs

## Definition

The risk that an LLM reveals confidential, proprietary, or personally identifiable information memorised from training data, leaked through fine-tuning, or fetched from connected internal services. The Samsung ChatGPT IP-leak incident is the canonical example of how easily sensitive data can cross the wrong trust boundary.
