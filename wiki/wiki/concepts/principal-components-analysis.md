# Principal Components Analysis

**Principal Components Analysis (PCA)** is eigen-decomposition of sample covariance to extract latent pervasive factors from large panels. Used to estimate factor spaces in approximate factor models.

## Sources

- [[sources/andreou-2020-mixed-frequency-macro-finance|Mixed-Frequency Macro-Finance Factor Models (2020)]] — central; used to estimate the common factors across macro and financial panels under both aggregation-first and PCA-first procedures
