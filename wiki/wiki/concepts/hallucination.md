# Hallucination

## Definition

An LLM output that is confidently presented but factually wrong or fabricated — e.g., nonexistent APIs, invented library functions, or incorrect imports — caused by lack of ground-truth verification, overfitting, biased training data, or extrapolation beyond the training distribution.
