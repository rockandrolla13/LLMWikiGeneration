# AI Engineering

## Definition

The discipline of building applications on top of readily available foundation models, distinguished from traditional ML engineering by its emphasis on model adaptation (prompting, RAG, finetuning, agents) rather than feature engineering and from-scratch training. Huyen positions it as the spiritual sequel to ML systems engineering.
