# GraphRAG

## Definition

A RAG variant that augments or replaces flat embedding retrieval with a knowledge graph extracted from the corpus, enabling multi-hop reasoning and structural retrieval. Popularized by Microsoft and adopted by graph database vendors to address limitations of similarity-only retrieval.
