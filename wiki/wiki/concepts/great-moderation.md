# Great Moderation

**Great Moderation** is the period of reduced US macroeconomic volatility (roughly mid-1980s to 2007). Used as a regime breakpoint when testing for structural change in common factor loadings.

## Sources

- [[sources/andreou-2020-mixed-frequency-macro-finance|Mixed-Frequency Macro-Finance Factor Models (2020)]] — supporting; serves as the breakpoint for the subperiod analysis showing a common macro-finance factor pre- and post-moderation but not in the full sample
