# Thoughts on the Market: Can $2 Trillion Flatten the Unemployment Curve?

**Authors:** Michael D Zezas, Ellen Zentner · **Year:** 2020 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

Morgan Stanley 'Thoughts on the Market' podcast (March 27, 2020) featuring strategist Michael Zezas and Chief US Economist Ellen Zentner. With a record 3.28 million workers filing for unemployment, they weigh the potential effects of the roughly $2 trillion fiscal package then before Congress. The discussion centers on whether the fiscal response can blunt the surge in unemployment amid the COVID-19 shock.

## Key Claims

1. A record 3.28 million workers filed for unemployment, underscoring the severity of the COVID-19 labor market shock.
2. A roughly $2 trillion fiscal package before Congress is assessed for its potential to flatten the unemployment curve.

## Questions Raised

- Can a $2 trillion fiscal package meaningfully flatten the unemployment curve?
