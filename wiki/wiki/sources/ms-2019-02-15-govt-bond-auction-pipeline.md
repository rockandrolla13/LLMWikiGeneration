# Government Bond Auction Pipeline | The Month Ahead

**Authors:** Matthew Hornbach, David Harris, Robert J Brown · **Year:** 2019 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

A supply-and-redemption review of government bond markets across the US, UK, euro area, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and Japan over the next month. For the coming week the team expects ~EUR23bn euro-area issuance against EUR0.6bn coupons and EUR16bn redemptions, a GBP1.5bn UKT 2057 tap, a new $8bn 30y US TIPS, and a JPY1000bn 20y JGB. Over the coming five weeks the eurozone is projected to see about EUR86bn of supply, resulting in a small net cash surplus of EUR1.3bn.

## Key Claims

1. Euro-area issuance of ~EUR23bn is expected next week versus EUR0.6bn coupons and EUR16bn redemptions, above the EUR16.4bn three-year average.
2. A new 30y US TIPS will be issued for $8bn, settling alongside new 2y, 5y, and 7y supply.
3. Eurozone supply over the coming five weeks is estimated at ~EUR86bn, producing a net cash surplus of EUR1.3bn.
4. Japan will issue JPY1000bn of 20y JGB against JPY4bn coupons and no redemptions.
