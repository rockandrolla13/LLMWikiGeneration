# Correction: Global Futures Rolls Report: Eurex Futures Rolls - Mar 19 / Jun 19 Roll

**Authors:** Robert J Brown · **Year:** 2019 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

A Global Bond Futures Strategy note presenting views on Eurex futures contract rolls for the March-to-June 2019 cycle across Schatz, Bobl, Bund, Buxl, and BTP contracts. It analyzes fundamental drivers of each roll, investor positioning, delivery optionality, and relative value versus model-implied fair value. The note is a republished correction that revised the fair-value estimate of the roll in Exhibit 8 and accompanying text.

## Key Claims

1. The Schatz roll is rated neutral as the new March 2021 CTD issuance complicates fair-value estimation and obscures any directional signal.
2. Bobl and Buxl rolls are mildly bearish given long positioning bias that could create cheapening pressure.
3. The Bund roll is neutral with no substantial optionality and no significant positioning bias.
4. The BTP roll is mildly bullish, supported by historical richening into delivery and a slight bearish positioning bias.
