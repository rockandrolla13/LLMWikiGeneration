# European High Yield Strategy Monthly Leveraged Finance Playbook — Returns

**Authors:** Andrew Sheets, Phanikiran Naraparaju, Serena Tang, Jonathan Graber, Joerund Holterud Aarsnes · **Year:** 2012 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

Monthly leveraged finance playbook for European high yield, reporting that the asset class returned 1.8% over the past month, led by beta, with CCCs returning 4.2% and industrials outperforming other non-financials. The trailing 12-month European speculative-grade default rate declined to 2.7% at end-January per Moody's. The report tracks index performance, cash and CDS indices, single-name out/underperformers, leveraged finance issuance and maturities, cash-CDS basis outliers, and distressed bonds.

## Key Claims

1. European high yield returned 1.8% over the past month, led by beta, with CCCs returning 4.2%.
2. The trailing 12-month European speculative-grade default rate declined to 2.7% at end-January per Moody's data.
3. In CDS, CWLN and MESSA outperformed while TKAGR underperformed; in cash, CMACG and PAJFP outperformed while IDEABB and CEDC underperformed.
