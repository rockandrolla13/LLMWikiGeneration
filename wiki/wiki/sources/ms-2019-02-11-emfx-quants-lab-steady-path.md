# EMFX Quant's Lab: Steady Path

**Authors:** Andres Jaime · **Year:** 2019 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

A periodic EM Quant Strategy note reporting the latest readings of Morgan Stanley's EMFX systematic models. The Volatility Risk Premia (VIRP) high-beta signal held at +4, pointing to continued medium-term momentum in EM as the Fed's dovish stance keeps volatility subdued, a backdrop historically favorable for EM as a carry asset class. The VIRP model remained long BRL, TRY, RUB and ZAR versus MYR, INR, IDR and THB, while the regime-switching model stalled but stayed firmly in a 'risk neutral' regime. The models suggest EM still has room to rally despite a possible pause.

## Key Claims

1. The VIRP high-beta signal remained at +4, indicating continued medium-term EM momentum supported by subdued volatility.
2. The model is positioned long BRL, TRY, RUB and ZAR versus MYR, INR, IDR and THB, with THB and IDR double-underweight on overbought two-factor signals.
3. The regime-switching model (RSM) stalled after four weeks of decline but remained firmly in the 'risk neutral' regime.
