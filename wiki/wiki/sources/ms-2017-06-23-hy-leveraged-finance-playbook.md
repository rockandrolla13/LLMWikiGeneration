# European High Yield Strategy Monthly Leveraged Finance Playbook — Performance

**Authors:** Srikanth Sankaran, Max Blass, Aron Becker · **Year:** 2017 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

A monthly European high yield strategy data note from Morgan Stanley covering performance, supply and demand, and defaults. EUR HY total returns continued to increase and stood at 3.4% YTD, with single-Bs outperforming and the B-BB basis compressing by another 35bp. European HY issuance fell ~15% month-on-month to EUR5.2bln in June, while HY mutual funds saw modest net inflows providing technical support. Trailing 12-month issuer-weighted default rates in European HY remained stable at 2.5% and Moody's global senior unsecured recovery rates reached 50%, the highest since late 2010.

## Key Claims

1. EUR HY total returns reached 3.4% YTD, with single-Bs outperforming and the B-BB basis compressing by 35bp.
2. European HY issuance fell ~15% month-on-month to EUR5.2bln in June while funds saw modest net inflows.
3. Trailing 12-month issuer-weighted European HY default rates remained stable at 2.5%, and senior unsecured recovery rates rose to 50%, the highest since late 2010.
