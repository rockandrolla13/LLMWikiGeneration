# Advanced Analytics and Learning on Temporal Data (AALTD 2024)

**Editors:** Vincent Lemaire, Georgiana Ifrim, Anthony Bagnall, Thomas Guyet, Simon Malinowski, Patrick Schäfer, Romain Tavenard

**Year:** 2024

**Venue:** Springer *Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence* 15433. 9th ECML PKDD Workshop AALTD 2024, Vilnius, Lithuania, September 9-13, 2024. 156 pages.

## Summary

Revised selected papers from the 9th AALTD workshop, co-located with ECML PKDD 2024. AALTD is a long-running workshop series on time-series machine learning, covering classification, forecasting, change-point detection, anomaly detection, and increasingly **uncertainty quantification** including conformal prediction on temporal data.

Workshop proceedings of this kind contain many independent contributions, often early-stage or applied. This page indexes the volume as a single source; individual chapters can be promoted to their own source pages when specific contributions prove load-bearing.

## Scope

Topics typically covered in the AALTD series:
- Time-series classification benchmarks (Bagnall's UCR/UEA archive lineage).
- Symbolic representations (SAX, SFA, BOSS family).
- Deep learning for time series (InceptionTime, ROCKET, transformers).
- Change-point and concept-drift detection.
- Anomaly detection on streaming data.
- **Conformal prediction for time series** — increasingly featured in recent AALTD editions, overlapping with the canon in [[sources/stocker-2025-conformal-timeseries-intro|Stocker et al. 2025]].
