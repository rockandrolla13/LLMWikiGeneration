# Attention Is All You Need

## Summary

The Transformer architecture relies entirely on [[self-attention]] mechanisms. This paper introduced the foundation for modern LLMs.

## Key Claims

1. **Attention replaces recurrence** - Self-attention provides sufficient modeling capacity
2. **Parallelization** - Transformers can be parallelized across positions
3. **SOTA results** - Best BLEU scores on translation
