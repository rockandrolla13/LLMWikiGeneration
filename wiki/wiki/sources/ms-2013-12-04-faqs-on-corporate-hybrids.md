# FAQs on Hybrids — Hybrids Monitor and Relative Value (Corporate Hybrids Playbook)

**Authors:** Andrew Sheets, Phanikiran Naraparaju, Serena Tang, Shrina B Poojara · **Year:** 2013 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

The Corporate Hybrids Playbook answers key investor questions on European corporate hybrid bonds covering structure, rating-agency methodology, valuation frameworks, and spread sensitivity. It updates price levels and valuation metrics, and tracks the hybrids universe with a Corporate Hybrids Monitor and relative-value scatters identifying opportunities. The report notes that European corporate hybrid new issuance broke a record in 2013. A bond-by-bond 'cheat sheet' maps key structures and terms of each security.

## Key Claims

1. European corporate hybrid new issuance broke a record in 2013.
2. The Corporate Hybrids Monitor reports hybrid spreads, hybrid-to-senior spread pickup, and up/down convexity (price return from 100bp tightening vs widening) across the European universe.
3. FAQs address hybrid structure, rating-agency equity-credit methodology, valuation frameworks, and spread sensitivity.
