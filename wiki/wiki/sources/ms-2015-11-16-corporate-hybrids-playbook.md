# European Credit Strategy: Corporate Hybrids Playbook

**Authors:** Srikanth Sankaran, Max Blass, Aron Becker · **Year:** 2015 · **Venue:** Morgan Stanley Research · **Type:** report

## Summary

Morgan Stanley provides a relative-value playbook for European corporate hybrid bonds, comparing current valuations against historical spreads and yields. The note reviews particularly sharp 2015 price swings that distorted the relationship between hybrids and senior bonds, including S&P's downward revision of the equity content of several hybrids. It includes a hybrid monitor, a features 'cheat sheet' of key structures and terms, and FAQs on hybrid structure, rating methodology, valuation frameworks and spread sensitivity.

## Key Claims

1. Average hybrid spread to senior bonds remained near recent peaks at the time of writing.
2. 2015 price swings in hybrids were particularly sharp, significantly distorting their relationship versus senior bonds.
3. S&P revised downward the equity content assigned to several corporate hybrids, affecting their treatment.
4. Relative value within European corporate hybrids is tracked via a hybrid monitor and a structure/terms cheat sheet.
