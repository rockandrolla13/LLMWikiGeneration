# Isaac Gibbs

**Isaac Gibbs** is the originator of [[concepts/adaptive-conformal-inference|Adaptive Conformal Inference (ACI)]] and most of its modern extensions. PhD student of Emmanuel Candès at Stanford.

## Affiliation

- Stanford University, Department of Statistics. PhD candidate / postdoc.

## Publications in This Wiki

- [[sources/gibbs-2021-aci]] — first author. The original ACI paper introducing the single-parameter `α_t` update.
- [[sources/gibbs-2024-online-aci]] — first author. DtACI with dynamic-regret bounds.
- [[sources/gibbs-2023-conditional-guarantees]] — first author. Best-effort PAC-style conditional coverage via covariate-shift classes.

## Co-authors in This Wiki

- [[entities/emmanuel-candes]] — advisor; co-author on all three papers.
- [[entities/john-cherian]] — co-author on the conditional-guarantees paper.
