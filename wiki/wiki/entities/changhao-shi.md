# Changhao Shi

**Affiliation:** University of California San Diego
**Degree:** PhD in Electrical Engineering (Machine Learning and Data Science), 2025
**Supervisor:** [[entities/gal-mishne|Gal Mishne]]

## Education

- **2018-2025:** PhD, University of California San Diego
- **2014-2018:** Bachelor of Science, Beihang University

## Research Focus

Changhao Shi's research focuses on [[concepts/graph-laplacian|graph Laplacian]] learning and [[concepts/graph-signal-processing|graph signal processing]], with particular emphasis on:
- Graph inference from non-Gaussian data
- Product graph structures
- Adversarial robustness via manifold geometry

## Doctoral Dissertation

**Title:** [[sources/shi-2024-graph-laplacian-learning|Generalizing Graph Laplacian Learning: A Graph Signal Processing Perspective]]
**Year:** 2024

### Key Contributions

1. **GLEN:** Graph Learning with Exponential family Noise - extends graph inference beyond Gaussian assumptions

2. **MWGL:** Multi-Way Graph Learning for tensor data on Cartesian product graphs

3. **KSGL:** Kronecker Structured Graph Learning with theoretical consistency guarantees

4. **SOAP:** Self-supervised Online Adversarial Purification for deep learning robustness

## Publications
