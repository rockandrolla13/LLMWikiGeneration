# Simone Vantini

**Simone Vantini** is a statistician working on [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]], [[concepts/functional-data-analysis|functional data analysis]], and nonparametric inference for complex data.

## Affiliation

- MOX – Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano, Italy.

## Research Focus

- Conformal prediction for functional and multivariate-functional data (Diquigiovanni-Fontana-Vantini 2021 series).
- Nonparametric inference under exchangeability.
- Applications to electricity demand and energy markets.
