# Michael I. Jordan

**Michael I. Jordan** is the Pehong Chen Distinguished Professor at UC Berkeley with joint appointments in EECS and Statistics. One of the most-cited figures in machine learning and statistical learning theory. Senior co-author on the Berkeley CP-vision and risk-control line.

## Affiliation

- Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences and Department of Statistics, UC Berkeley.

## Research Focus

Graphical models, variational inference, statistical learning theory, optimisation, computational game theory, and more recently distribution-free uncertainty quantification and risk control via [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]].
