# Evan Patterson

**Evan Patterson** is a statistician and applied category theorist who co-authored the original [[concepts/conformalized-quantile-regression|CQR]] paper during his time at Stanford.

## Affiliation

- Stanford University, Department of Statistics (at time of CQR paper).
- Later: Topos Institute, Berkeley (applied category theory and statistical informatics).
