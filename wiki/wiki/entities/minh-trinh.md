# Minh Trinh

**Minh Trinh** was a quantitative credit researcher at [[entities/lehman-brothers|Lehman Brothers]], where he co-developed the LEVER framework for assessing leveraged buyout and recapitalization risk.

## Research Contributions

### LEVER Framework (2006)

Co-authored with [[entities/bodha-bhattacharya|Bodha Bhattacharya]], the LEVER framework introduced:

- **Firm LEVER-Score:** Company-specific LBO/recap risk (0-10 scale)
- **Macro LEVER-Score:** Market environment favorability
- Three component scores: Valuation, Operations, Execution

The framework demonstrated strong predictive power in backtests (1995-2005) and identified several 2005 underperformers before their credit deterioration.
