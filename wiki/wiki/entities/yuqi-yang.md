# Yuqi Yang

**Yuqi Yang** is a researcher working on conformal prediction and uncertainty quantification methods.

## Research Contributions

### Multi-Distribution Robust Conformal Prediction
Co-developed [[concepts/multi-distribution-robust-cp|Multi-Distribution Robust Conformal Prediction]] (MDCP) with [[entities/ying-jin|Ying Jin]], addressing the problem of constructing prediction sets valid across multiple heterogeneous data sources.

The method introduces:
- [[concepts/max-p-aggregation|Max-p aggregation]] for uniform coverage
- Optimal score learning via dual optimization
- Applications to fairness and distribution robustness
