# Luca Bosatta

**Luca Bosatta** is Managing Director and Head of Risk Modelling at [[entities/ubs-delta|UBS Delta]].

## Research Contributions

### Next-Generation Credit Curves (2012)

Co-authored with [[entities/lindsey-matthews|Lindsey Matthews]], presented UBS Delta's D-Curves methodology:

- Unified hazard-rate curves combining bonds and CDS
- Joint estimation across multiple currencies
- Market surfaces reducing artificial rating-migration jumps
- Bond-implied CDS derivation for issuers without traded CDS
