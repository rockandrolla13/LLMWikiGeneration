# Lindsey Matthews

**Lindsey Matthews** is Managing Director and Head of Client Development at [[entities/ubs-delta|UBS Delta]].

## Research Contributions

### Next-Generation Credit Curves (2012)

Co-authored with [[entities/luca-bosatta|Luca Bosatta]], presented UBS Delta's D-Curves methodology:

- Unified hazard-rate curves across bonds and CDS
- Multi-currency joint estimation
- Market surfaces for sector/rating combinations
- Market-implied ratings framework
