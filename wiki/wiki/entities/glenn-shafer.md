# Glenn Shafer

**Glenn Shafer** is a statistician and probabilist, co-inventor of [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]] (with [[entities/vladimir-vovk|Vovk]] and [[entities/alexander-gammerman|Gammerman]]) and the person who **coined the term "conformal predictor"**.

## Affiliation

- Faculty at Rutgers Business School, Newark.
- Known beyond CP for Dempster-Shafer theory of belief functions and for game-theoretic probability (developed jointly with Vovk; book *Probability and Finance*, 2001).

## Research Focus

- Foundations of probability (game-theoretic alternative to measure-theoretic).
- Belief functions and evidence combination (Dempster-Shafer theory).
- Conformal prediction, particularly its on-line formulation and the connection to martingales and Cournot's principle.
