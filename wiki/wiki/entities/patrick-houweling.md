# Patrick Houweling

## Overview

Senior researcher at Robeco Investment Research specializing in corporate bond factor investing. Co-author of influential papers establishing factor premiums in corporate bond markets.

## Affiliation

**Robeco Investment Research**
Rotterdam, Netherlands

## Research Focus

- Factor investing in corporate bonds
- Size, Low-Risk, Value, and Momentum factors
- Multi-factor portfolio construction
- Stock-to-bond momentum spillover

## Key Contributions

### Factor Investing Framework
Established that Size, Low-Risk, Value, and Momentum generate significant alphas in corporate bonds - first comprehensive evidence on Size factor in bond markets.

### Momentum Spillover Research
Documented and improved momentum spillover from equities to corporate bonds, showing residual momentum dramatically improves performance.
