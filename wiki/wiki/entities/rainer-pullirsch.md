# Rainer Pullirsch

**Rainer Pullirsch** is a credit risk researcher who worked at Bank Austria Creditanstalt, focusing on credit-spread curve estimation and risk measurement.

## Research Contributions

### Credit-Spread Risk Measurement (2006)

Published "Measuring Credit-Spread Risk on a Single Issuer Basis" in Wirtschaft und Management (November 2006), which presented:

- Method for estimating zero-coupon credit-spread curves from bond quotations
- Numerical stability analysis and grid point selection
- **Residual variance** methodology to capture bond-specific behavior
- Three-level mapping procedure for illiquid issuers
- VaR calculation framework for credit portfolios

## Key Innovations

**Numerical Stability Control:**
- Maximum sensitivity measure Δ for grid point selection
- Automatic grid point reduction when stability criterion not met
- Trade-off management between stability and pricing accuracy

**Residual Variance:**
- Captures currency differences in bond trading
- Accounts for liquidity and bond-specific factors
- Time-weighted variance calculation (λ = 0.97)
