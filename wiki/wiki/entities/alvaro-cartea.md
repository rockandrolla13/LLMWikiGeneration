# Álvaro Cartea

## Overview

Álvaro Cartea is a Professor of Mathematical Finance at the **University of Oxford** (Saïd Business School and Mathematical Institute) and previously at **University College London**. He is a leading researcher in algorithmic trading, optimal execution, and market making.

## Research Contributions

### Optimal Execution
Cartea has made significant contributions to [[concepts/optimal-execution]] theory:
- Integration of limit and market orders
- Risk-adjusted expected PnL frameworks
- Ambiguity aversion in execution strategies

### Market Making
Key contributions to [[concepts/market-making]] models:
- Alternative objective functions to CARA utility
- Incorporation of alpha signals
- Multi-asset extensions

## Major Publications

### Books
Co-author of **"Algorithmic and High-Frequency Trading"** (Cambridge University Press, 2015) with Sebastian Jaimungal and José Penalva - a foundational textbook in the field.

### Papers in This Wiki
- [[sources/cartea-2015-optimal-execution]] - Optimal execution with limit and market orders
- [[sources/cartea-2025-statistical-predictions-trading|Statistical Predictions of Trading Strategies in Electronic Markets (2025)]] — algorithm-level order-flow prediction and trader behavioral clustering on Euronext Amsterdam

## Key Methodological Contributions

- **Risk-adjusted PnL framework**: Alternative to CARA utility with running inventory penalty
- **Trigger boundaries**: Optimal timing for market order execution
- **Fill probability modeling**: Exponential dependence on order depth
