# Aaditya Ramdas

**Aaditya Ramdas** is Associate Professor at Carnegie Mellon University with joint appointments in Statistics & Data Science and Machine Learning. Known for work on sequential hypothesis testing, anytime-valid inference, and [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]] under non-exchangeability.

## Affiliation

- Carnegie Mellon University, Department of Statistics & Data Science and Machine Learning Department.

## Research Focus

Sequential testing, anytime-valid inference, e-values, conformal prediction beyond exchangeability, FDR control, multi-armed bandits.
