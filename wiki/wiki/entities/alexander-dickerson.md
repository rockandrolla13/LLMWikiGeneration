# Alexander Dickerson

## Overview

Academic researcher at UNSW Business School specializing in corporate bond pricing and market microstructure. Known for critical methodological contributions identifying biases in corporate bond research.

## Affiliation

**UNSW Business School**
Sydney, Australia

## Research Focus

- Corporate bond market microstructure
- Transaction cost analysis
- Bid-ask bias correction
- Empirical research methodology

## Key Contributions

### Bond Research Methodology
Authored critical paper demonstrating that large abnormal returns in corporate bond strategies are often artifacts of:
1. Market microstructure noise in TRACE prices
2. Look-ahead bias from ex-post data filtering

### Open Science Resources
- **openbondassetpricing.com**: Public website with corrected data and code
- **PyBondLab**: Open-source Python software for bond portfolio construction
