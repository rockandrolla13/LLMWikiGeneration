# Sebastian Jaimungal

## Overview

Sebastian Jaimungal is a Professor at the **Department of Statistical Sciences, University of Toronto**. He is a prolific researcher in quantitative finance with expertise in algorithmic trading, mean-field games, and machine learning applications in finance.

## Research Contributions

### Algorithmic Trading
Jaimungal has made foundational contributions to:
- [[concepts/optimal-execution]] with various order types
- [[concepts/market-making]] models with realistic features
- Reinforcement learning for trading

### Mean-Field Games
Pioneer in applying mean-field game theory to:
- Large population trading games
- Market impact modeling
- Nash equilibria in trading

## Major Publications

### Books
Co-author of **"Algorithmic and High-Frequency Trading"** (Cambridge University Press, 2015) with Álvaro Cartea and José Penalva.

### Papers in This Wiki
- [[sources/cartea-2015-optimal-execution]] - Optimal execution with limit and market orders

## Methodological Innovations

- **Mixed order strategies**: Optimal combination of limit and market orders
- **Ambiguity aversion**: Robust strategies under model uncertainty
- **Machine learning integration**: RL methods for trading problems
