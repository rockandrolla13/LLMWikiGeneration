# Bodha Bhattacharya

**Bodhaditya (Bodha) Bhattacharya** was a quantitative credit researcher at [[entities/lehman-brothers|Lehman Brothers]], where he co-developed the LEVER framework for assessing leveraged buyout and recapitalization risk.

## Research Contributions

### LEVER Framework (2006)

Co-authored with [[entities/minh-trinh|Minh Trinh]], the LEVER framework introduced:

- **Firm LEVER-Score:** Company-specific LBO/recap risk (0-10 scale)
- **Macro LEVER-Score:** Market environment favorability
- Systematic approach to identifying LBO candidates
