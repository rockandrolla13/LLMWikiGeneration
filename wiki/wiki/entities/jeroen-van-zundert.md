# Jeroen van Zundert

## Overview

Researcher at Robeco Investment Research focusing on factor strategies in corporate bond markets. Co-author of foundational research on bond factor premiums and momentum spillover.

## Affiliation

**Robeco Investment Research**
Rotterdam, Netherlands

## Research Focus

- Corporate bond factor investing
- Momentum strategies
- Cross-asset spillover effects
- Risk factor exposures

## Key Contributions

### Bond Factor Research
Co-established empirical evidence for Size, Low-Risk, Value, and Momentum factor premiums in corporate bonds.

### Residual Momentum Innovation
Demonstrated that using residual (firm-specific) equity returns for momentum spillover dramatically reduces risk and improves Sharpe ratios.
