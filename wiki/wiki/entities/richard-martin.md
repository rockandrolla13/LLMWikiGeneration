# Richard J. Martin

**Affiliation:** Department of Mathematics, Imperial College London

**Research Areas:** Fixed income, credit spreads, quantitative finance

## Key Works in Wiki

- [[sources/martin-2024-credit-curve|The Credit Curve Spread I (2024)]] — Fundamental concepts for credit spread curve construction

## Contributions

Richard Martin's work focuses on practical quantitative methods for fixed income investing, bridging academic theory with market practice. His credit curve paper is notable for:

- Critiquing fundamental errors in academic literature (including Duffie & Singleton)
- Developing correct valuation frameworks for risky bonds
- Explaining the par/non-par problem in bond pricing
- Providing methods for carry, rolldown, and relative value calculation
