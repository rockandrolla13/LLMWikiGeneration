# Mike Ludkovski

**Affiliation:** Department of Statistics and Applied Probability, UC Santa Barbara

**Research Areas:** Gaussian processes, actuarial science, mortality modelling, machine learning

## Key Works in Wiki

- [[sources/huynh-2021-mogp-longevity|Multi-output Gaussian Processes for Multi-population Longevity Modelling (2021)]] — MOGP framework for mortality

## Research Focus

Mike Ludkovski's research applies machine learning methods to actuarial problems:

- **Gaussian process regression** for mortality surfaces
- **Multi-output GPs** for joint population modelling
- **Spatial statistical methods** in longevity analysis
- **Bayesian uncertainty quantification** in forecasting

## Contributions

Pioneer in applying modern ML/GP methods to traditional actuarial problems, particularly multi-population mortality modelling where his MOGP approach scales to 10+ populations while maintaining coherent forecasts.
