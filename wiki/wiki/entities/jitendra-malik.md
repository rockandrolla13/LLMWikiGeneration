# Jitendra Malik

**Jitendra Malik** is Arthur J. Chick Professor of EECS at UC Berkeley. A foundational figure in computer vision. Co-author on the Berkeley CP-for-vision papers (RAPS, RCPS) bringing rigorous distribution-free guarantees to image classification.

## Affiliation

- Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences, UC Berkeley.

## Research Focus

Computer vision (segmentation, object recognition, scene understanding), perceptual organisation, deep learning, and more recently distribution-free uncertainty quantification for vision models.

## Publications in This Wiki

- [[sources/angelopoulos-2021-raps]] — co-author. RAPS conformal classification at ImageNet scale.
- [[sources/bates-2021-rcps]] — co-author. Risk-Controlling Prediction Sets with applications to segmentation.
