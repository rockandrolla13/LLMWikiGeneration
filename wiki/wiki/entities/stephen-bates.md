# Stephen Bates

**Stephen Bates** is a statistician working on [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]], [[concepts/conformal-risk-control|risk control]], and distribution-free statistics.

## Affiliation

- Faculty at MIT (EECS / IDSS), previously postdoctoral at UC Berkeley and Stanford.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of "[[sources/angelopoulos-2022-gentle-intro|A Gentle Introduction to Conformal Prediction]]" with [[entities/anastasios-angelopoulos|Angelopoulos]].
- Co-developer of [[concepts/conformal-risk-control|conformal risk control]] for general bounded losses.
- Co-developer of [[concepts/learn-then-test|Learn Then Test]] for non-monotone risks.
- Co-developer of [[concepts/regularized-adaptive-prediction-sets|RAPS]] for adaptive classification sets.
