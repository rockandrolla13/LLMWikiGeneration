# Souhaib Ben Taieb

**Souhaib Ben Taieb** is a researcher in forecasting, time-series machine learning, and [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]] for sequential data.

## Affiliation

- Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), Abu Dhabi, UAE.
- University of Mons, Belgium.

## Research Focus

Probabilistic forecasting, hierarchical time series, deep learning for forecasting, and post-hoc calibration of forecast uncertainty.
