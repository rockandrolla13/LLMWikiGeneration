# Matteo Fontana

**Matteo Fontana** is a statistician working on [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]], including functional and time-series extensions.

## Affiliation

- Currently: European Commission Joint Research Centre (JRC), Ispra.
- Previously: MOX – Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano, and Royal Holloway University of London (joint with Vovk's CP group).
