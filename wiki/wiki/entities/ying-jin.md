# Ying Jin

**Ying Jin** is a researcher in statistics and data science at the University of Pennsylvania, specializing in conformal prediction, uncertainty quantification, and distributionally robust methods.

## Affiliation

- Department of Statistics and Data Science, University of Pennsylvania
- Email: yjinstat@wharton.upenn.edu

## Research Contributions

### Multi-Distribution Robust Conformal Prediction
Co-developed [[concepts/multi-distribution-robust-cp|Multi-Distribution Robust Conformal Prediction]] (MDCP) with [[entities/yuqi-yang|Yuqi Yang]], a framework for prediction sets with uniform validity across multiple heterogeneous distributions.

Key contributions:
- [[concepts/max-p-aggregation|Max-p aggregation]] scheme for finite-sample uniform validity
- Optimality characterization of aggregated prediction sets
- End-to-end learning algorithms for efficient conformity scores

### Broader Conformal Prediction Research
Jin has contributed to various aspects of conformal prediction, including:
- Sensitivity to distribution shift
- Conditional coverage
- Distributionally robust methods

## Key Publications

- **Multi-Distribution Robust Conformal Prediction** (2026, arXiv:2601.02998) - With Yang
- Related work on sensitivity analysis and conditional conformal prediction
