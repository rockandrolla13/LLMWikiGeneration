## Biography

Jorge Zubelli is a mathematician with expertise in inverse problems, mathematical finance, and partial differential equations. He has held positions at IMPA (Instituto Nacional de Matematica Pura e Aplicada) in Brazil and KAUST (King Abdullah University of Science and Technology).

## Research Contributions

### Mathematical Finance

1. **Pairs Trading**: Collaborated on developing state-space model approaches to pairs trading with probabilistic trading rules ([[sources/moura-2016-pairs-trading-kalman|de Moura et al. 2016]])

2. **Calibration Problems**: Applied inverse problem techniques to financial model calibration

3. **Option Pricing**: Research on derivatives pricing under various model specifications

### Broader Research Areas

- Inverse problems in imaging and tomography
- Partial differential equations
- Computational methods for finance
