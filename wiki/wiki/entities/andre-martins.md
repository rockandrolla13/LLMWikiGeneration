# Andre F.T. Martins

**Andre F.T. Martins** is a prominent researcher in natural language processing and machine learning, with significant contributions to structured prediction, attention mechanisms, and uncertainty quantification.

## Affiliation

- Unbabel (VP of AI Research)
- Instituto de Telecomunicações, Lisbon
- Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa (Lisbon ELLIS Unit)

## Research Focus

Martins' research spans:
- Structured prediction and sequence-to-sequence models
- Sparse attention mechanisms
- Uncertainty quantification for NLP
- Machine translation quality estimation

## Key Contributions

### Non-Exchangeable Conformal Risk Control

As senior author on [[sources/farinhas-2024-non-exchangeable-crc|Farinhas et al. (2024)]], Martins contributed to extending conformal risk control to non-exchangeable data settings, with applications to open-domain question answering.

### Sparse Transformers

Martins' lab developed entmax and related sparse attention mechanisms that enable interpretable and efficient transformers.
