# Henry Lam

**Henry Lam** is a faculty member at the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, Columbia University, specializing in uncertainty quantification, simulation, and operations research.

## Affiliation

- Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, Columbia University, New York, USA

## Research Focus

Lam's research focuses on:
- Uncertainty quantification in simulation and machine learning
- Robust optimization
- Distributionally robust methods
- High-confidence prediction intervals

## Key Contributions

### Extreme Conformal Prediction
Co-developed [[sources/pasche-2025-extreme-conformal|extreme conformal prediction]] with Pasche and Engelke, enabling reliable prediction intervals at very high confidence levels for high-impact events such as floods and financial crises.
