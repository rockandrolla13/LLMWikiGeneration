# Rina Foygel Barber

**Rina Foygel Barber** is Professor of Statistics at the University of Chicago. She is one of the central figures of the modern [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]] theoretical wave, with foundational work on [[concepts/jackknife-plus|Jackknife+]] and [[concepts/non-exchangeable-conformal-prediction|non-exchangeable CP]].

## Affiliation

- Department of Statistics, University of Chicago.
- Alfred P. Sloan Fellow.
