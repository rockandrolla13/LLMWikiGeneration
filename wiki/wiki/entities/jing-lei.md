# Jing Lei

**Jing Lei** is Professor of Statistics at Carnegie Mellon University. A foundational contributor to the CMU [[concepts/conformal-prediction|conformal prediction]] school, with influential work on split conformal regression and locally-adaptive variants.

## Affiliation

- Department of Statistics & Data Science, Carnegie Mellon University.

## Research Focus

Distribution-free predictive inference, conformal prediction, high-dimensional regression, change-point detection, network analysis.

## Publications in This Wiki

- [[sources/lei-2018-distribution-free-regression]] — first author. The pre-Angelopoulos canonical CP-for-regression reference.
