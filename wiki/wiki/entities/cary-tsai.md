# Cary Chi-Liang Tsai

**Affiliation:** Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science, Simon Fraser University

**Research Areas:** Actuarial science, mortality modelling, credibility theory, longevity risk

## Key Works in Wiki

- [[sources/tsai-2020-hierarchical-mortality|Hierarchical Credibility Theory for Multi-Country Mortality (2020)]] — Applying credibility theory to mortality

## Research Focus

Cary Tsai's research bridges property/casualty credibility methods with life/longevity modelling:

- **Hierarchical credibility models** for multi-population mortality
- **Lee-Carter model extensions** using credibility theory
- **Longevity risk** management and forecasting
- **Multi-population mortality** correlation structures

## Key Contributions

- Extended Bühlmann credibility theory to mortality modelling
- Demonstrated hierarchical credibility outperforms Lee-Carter variants
- Developed stochastic versions for predictive intervals
