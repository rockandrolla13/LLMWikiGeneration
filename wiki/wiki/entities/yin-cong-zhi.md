# Yin-Cong Zhi

**Affiliation:** University of Oxford, St Anne's College
**Degree:** DPhil (2024)
**Supervisor:** [[entities/xiaowen-dong|Xiaowen Dong]]

## Research Focus

Yin-Cong Zhi's doctoral research focused on developing [[concepts/gaussian-processes|Gaussian processes]] for graph-structured data, combining probabilistic machine learning with [[concepts/graph-signal-processing|graph signal processing]] tools.

## Doctoral Thesis

**Title:** [[sources/zhi-2024-gaussian-processes-graphs|Gaussian Processes on Graphs]]
**Year:** 2024
**Institution:** University of Oxford

### Technical Contributions

1. **Spectral Kernel Learning:** Learning optimal kernels via their spectral function on the [[concepts/graph-laplacian|graph Laplacian]]

2. **Wavelet-based GPs:** Utilizing [[concepts/spectral-graph-wavelets|spectral graph wavelets]] for adaptive multi-scale modeling

3. **Transductive Kernels:** Unified kernel framework for attributed graphs combining node features and graph structure

4. **[[concepts/sheaf-laplacian|Sheaf Laplacian]] GPs:** Higher-order graph representations for improved semi-supervised classification
